Virtual Reality

In 2012 I joined the Oculus Rift kickstarter and I was blown away by what I was looking at, despite the low resolution. At that point I knew that I would create 3d content for virtual reality. I think the most impressive thing that can come in the next couple of years is a way to walk in these environments without bulky heavy treadmill equipment.
